Practical Safety Guidance

Essential tips to help everyone navigate Roblox confidently and securely

Account Security Basics

Always use a strong, unique password and enable 2-Step Verification (2SV) to protect your Roblox account from unauthorized access.

Respectful Communication

Treat every player with kindness. Use the Block and Report features immediately if you encounter harassment or inappropriate language.

Reporting Concerns

If you see something unsafe, use the Report Abuse tool located in the menu of every experience and player profile to alert our moderation team.

Recognizing Suspicious Behavior

Never share personal information like passwords, emails, or addresses. Be cautious of free Robux scams and phishing links promising easy rewards.

Guiding Younger Players

Help kids set up parental controls, review privacy settings, and encourage open conversations about their online interactions for a safer experience.
