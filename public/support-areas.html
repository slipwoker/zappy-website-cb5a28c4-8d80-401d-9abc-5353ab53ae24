Common Roblox support questions

Find quick answers to the most frequent issues players encounter. If you don't see your question here, our team is ready to help with any roblox support help you need.

I can't log into my account

If you're having trouble logging in, first ensure your username and password are correct. Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page to reset your credentials. Check your email's spam folder for the reset link, and make sure your account isn't locked due to multiple failed attempts.

How do I keep my account safe?

Protect your Roblox account by enabling 2-Step Verification from your account settings. Never share your password or browser cookies with anyone, and always log out when using shared devices. Use a strong, unique password that you don't use on other websites.

I noticed suspicious activity

If you see unauthorized purchases, missing items, or messages you didn't send, change your password immediately and enable 2-Step Verification. Contact Roblox support through the official help form to report the incident and request an account rollback if items were lost.

The app keeps crashing

App crashes are often caused by outdated software or insufficient device storage. Update your Roblox app to the latest version, clear the app cache, and restart your device. On PC, try reinstalling Roblox and updating your graphics drivers for better stability.

Parent concerns and controls

Parents can set up a PIN-protected parental controls menu to restrict chat, limit spending, and control which experiences their child can access. Visit the Account Restrictions section in Settings to customize age-appropriate boundaries and review the activity log regularly.

I don't know which category fits

Not sure where your issue belongs? Start by describing your problem in the general help search bar on the support page. The system will suggest relevant articles and categories. If you still need help contact roblox support by submitting a ticket under "Other" and our team will route it correctly.
